Ganga-House Surgeon

A house surgeon is a surgeon who works for a hospital and, when on duty, is available there. In the UK, a house surgeon is the senior member of the surgical house staff responsible for the execution of the orders of the attending surgeon, and who also substitutes when the attending surgeon is not present.

  1. Communication is an important part of any clinical practice
  2. Becoming a good surgeon is a lifelong process
  3. Discusses surgery results with patients and medical staff
